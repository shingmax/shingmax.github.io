"Every Life Matters, Every Action Counts."

At L.U.C.Y, we believe that every animal deserves a chance at a better life. Our mission is to ensure that all animals in Nepal receive the respect, love, and care they deserve.

"Because they can't speak, but we can act."

From rescuing strays to providing medical aid and fostering education about animal welfare, we are dedicated to making a lasting impact.

"Together, let's build a kinder world."

Join us in our journey to create a compassionate world for animals!

How You Can Help

  • Volunteering at our centers or events.
  • Donating to help us provide food, shelter, and medical aid.
  • Adopting or fostering animals in need.
  • Spreading the word about our mission and initiatives.

Together, we can create a brighter future for animals in Nepal. Thank you for being a part of our journey!
