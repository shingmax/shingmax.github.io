Our Services

"Every Life Matters, Every Action Counts."

How We Help

At L.U.C.Y, we are committed to transforming the lives of animals. Through our dedicated programs, we aim to create a safer, healthier, and happier world for them. Here's what we offer:

1. We Rescue

Our rescue team acts quickly to save injured, abandoned, and distressed animals. We ensure they receive immediate medical care and attention, followed by rehabilitation for a brighter future.

Highlights:

  • 24/7 emergency response.
  • Collaboration with local communities for effective rescues.
  • Care and rehabilitation at our centers.

2. We Help with Adoption

Adoption is at the heart of what we do. We work to find loving homes for animals that have been abandoned or rescued. Our adoption process ensures that every animal is matched with a caring family.

Why Adopt?

  • Give animals a second chance at life.
  • Reduce the number of stray animals.
  • Build a lifelong bond with a new companion.

3. We Vaccinate Street Animals

Vaccination is crucial for controlling diseases in both animals and humans. Our vaccination drives target street animals to ensure they remain healthy and reduce the risk of disease outbreaks.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Rabies prevention campaigns.
  • Routine health check-ups during vaccination drives.
  • Community education on the importance of animal vaccinations.

4. Low-Cost Medical Facilities

Our affordable medical services ensure that pets and stray animals receive essential care. From minor injuries to life-saving treatments, we are here to help.

Services Offered:

  • Vaccinations and routine check-ups.
  • Spay/neuter programs to control the animal population.
  • Emergency and long-term treatments.

5. Legal Team to Fight Animal Cruelty


Our legal team is dedicated to advocating for animals and ensuring justice for victims of cruelty. We take legal action against perpetrators and work to create a society where animal rights are respected.
What We Do:

  • Investigate cases of animal cruelty.
  • Work with law enforcement to prosecute offenders.
  • Raise awareness about animal rights and laws.
  • Lobby for stronger animal protection laws.
