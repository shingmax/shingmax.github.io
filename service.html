How We Help

At L.U.C.Y, we are committed to transforming the lives of animals. Through our dedicated programs, we aim to create a safer, healthier, and happier world for them. Here's what we offer:

We Rescue

Our rescue team acts quickly to save injured, abandoned, and distressed animals. We ensure they receive immediate medical care and rehabilitation for a brighter future.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Collaboration with local communities
  • Care and rehabilitation at our centers

We Help with Adoption

Adoption is at the heart of what we do. We match animals with loving homes, giving them a second chance at life.

  • Reduce stray animal populations
  • Ensure a brighter future for rescues
  • Build lifelong bonds

We Vaccinate Street Animals

Vaccination is crucial to controlling diseases. Our vaccination drives protect animals and communities alike.

  • Rabies prevention campaigns
  • Routine health check-ups
  • Community education

Low-Cost Medical Facilities

Affordable medical services ensure pets and stray animals receive essential care, from minor injuries to life-saving treatments.

  • Spay/neuter programs
  • Vaccinations and routine check-ups
  • Emergency treatments

Legal Team to Fight Animal Cruelty

Our legal team advocates for animals and ensures justice for victims of cruelty. We also work on strengthening animal rights laws.

  • Investigate animal cruelty cases
  • Collaborate with law enforcement
  • Raise awareness of animal rights

Join Us in Making a Difference

Be part of our mission to create a world where every animal is cared for and loved. Together, we can ensure a brighter future for animals in Nepal.
