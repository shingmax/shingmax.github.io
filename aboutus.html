About Us

Why

L.U.C.Y exists to address the suffering endured by animals who lack the power to control their lives. Neglect, cruelty, and ignorance combine to create a hostile environment for many creatures.

Nepal is a country where street animals often suffer due to lack of care and misinformation. Our goal is to reduce suffering by providing medical care, promoting adoption, conducting vaccination programs, and spreading awareness about animal rights.

Our Mission in Nepal

After years of political instability and the devastating 2015 earthquake, Nepal faces numerous challenges. Animal welfare remains a low priority, but L.U.C.Y is dedicated to creating change. Established to improve the welfare of street animals, our work spans rescuing, rehabilitating, and providing support to animals across the country.

Where We Work

L.U.C.Y operates from two primary bases in Nepal: Kathmandu and Pokhara. From these bases, we coordinate rescue operations, vaccination drives, and community outreach programs aimed at improving the lives of animals and educating communities.

Kathmandu

As Nepal's capital, Kathmandu is central to our operations. We work with local municipalities to provide animal control, rescue services, and medical care for street animals. Our team is dedicated to responding quickly and efficiently to calls for help.

Pokhara

Known for its scenic beauty, Pokhara is home to many stray animals in need of care. L.U.C.Y’s team in Pokhara focuses on vaccinations, adoption drives, and reducing the population of stray animals humanely through spay and neuter programs.

Outreach Work

In addition to our base locations, L.U.C.Y responds to municipalities across Nepal, assisting in managing stray animal populations and transitioning from culling to humane population control. Temporary camps provide spay/neuter services, vaccinations, and treatment for street animals on a large scale.
